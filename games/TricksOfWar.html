Tricks of War

Game Overview

Tricks of War is a game that exists out of the question, “Can we play multiple tricks at once?”. In the game, players are waging war throughout Europe during the Renaissance on multiple fronts while making pledges to their people about what kind of ruler they will be. Our suits represent our pledges with Military, Alchemy, Espionage, Culture, and Religion being the many different ways to wage war against your fellow rulers. Monarch cards are our trump suit. These cards are inspired and represented by 12 real life Renaissance monarchs. They come with an ability to affect the game and count as whatever suit you pledge to your people.

Key Features

Simultaneous Tricks

You aren't playing one "war," you’re playing several. You must decide where to commit your high cards and where to strategically concede.

Tactical Hand Storage

This is where the deck-building happens. The cards you win determine what can be saved for the next round. You are "recruiting" your future war pieces.

Public Pledges

At the start of the round, you bet on a suit you believe you will dominate. Fulfilling these political promises is the key to earning Influence Points.
