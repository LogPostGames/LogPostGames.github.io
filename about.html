About Us

Studio Overview

Log Post Games – Where strange ideas become memorable experiences.

Log Post Games is an independent game studio dedicated to crafting imaginative, story-driven adventures with heart, humor, and technical ambition. We believe in exploring the most interesting version of every idea and invite players to discover worlds that feel both surprising and deeply personal.

Who We Are
Jess Case is the founder and creative lead at Log Post Games. With a background in programming, design, and a lifelong love of storytelling, Jess brings a multidisciplinary approach to game development. Jess is passionate about building playful systems, experimenting with narrative, and fostering a studio culture of curiosity and collaboration.
Core Values
Commit to the Bit
If an idea is worth doing, it’s worth doing well. We chase the most interesting, surprising, or detailed version of a concept. We prioritize deep systems and careful execution over easy shortcuts.
Learn and Grow
Embrace failure and practice with purpose. We view every bug, scrapped feature, or design flaw as a chance to deepen our technical craft. Transparency about our challenges is part of the process.
Games Are Art
Experiences can elicit strong feelings, change hearts, and inspire new ideas. We design our games not just for fun, but to convey a deeper meaning, provoke thought, or spark an emotional response.
