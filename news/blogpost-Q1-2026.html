Lost Post Games: Blog Post - Q1 2026 Quarterly Review

April 1, 2026

Incoming Release

Here we are at the end of the first quarter of the year. Log Post Games has honestly been all over the place, but that’s changing! We are refocusing (yes again). Tricks of War is now the #1 priority project with a planned release date! (More on that later…) Let’s take a look at the current project lineup.

Captain’s Log: Holding Pattern

This project started small, but my hopes for it quickly grew too much. While a framework exists for the game, I simply can’t do everything I want in the game myself. I’ve decided to hold on Captain’s Log for now while I work on smaller projects and grow the studio’s funding. Don’t expect to hear anything on this front for a while, but hopefully it can become a bigger flagship project in the future!

Warp Step: New Project Reveal

I got excited last quarter. I announced the work on this and it still will be happening, but not yet and definitely not this year. This is a smaller project than Captain’s Log and is currently being considered the next (actually first) digital project for Log Post Games, and is receiving background work but is not the main priority.

Tricks of War: Launch Date!?

It’s been a minute since Tricks of War has been a focus, so a little reminder! Tricks of War is a trick-taking, deck-building board/card game inspired by the Renaissance and now featuring hand carved artwork. I got into linocut art at the beginning of the year, and, while I’m not a professional, it has all started to come together. I’m considering Tricks of War to be in an alpha phase with open playtesting happening right now!

- Jess Case, Founder of Log Post Games
