Announcing "Signal Lantern" – Our Next Big Adventure!

March 28, 2025

I am thrilled to announce the launch of the new independent video game development studio, Log Post Games! As a passionate solo developer and full-time engineer working on various VR projects, I'm looking to create interesting experiences.

The name Log Post Games is a blend of my love for engineering and my passion for writing. "Log" stands for the debug logs commonly used to tell developers information about what is happening in the game's systems, and "Post" is a playful nod to mail, representing the narrative side of the studio.

Currently, I'm hard at work on my first project, a narrative-driven game set on an abandoned spaceship. Expect an adventure full of mystery, intrigue, and unexpected twists as you explore the dark corridors and uncover the secrets about what happened on this ship.
